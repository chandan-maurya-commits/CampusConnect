About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a centralized academic management platform developed to streamline campus operations and reduce dependency on manual processes. It integrates academic tasks such as assignment management, club and event coordination, and placement drive updates into one unified system. By providing role-based access and personalized dashboards for students, faculty, and coordinators, CampusConnect enhances transparency, communication, and collaboration across the entire institution.
